## Applicable Standards & Regulations

### SOC 2 Type II
- **Edition:** AICPA Trust Services Criteria (2017, updated 2022)
- **Current as of:** January 2026
- **Canonical Source:** [AICPA Trust Services](https://www.aicpa.org/resources/article/soc-2-services)
- **Project Obligations:**
  - Implement security, availability, and confidentiality controls
  - Maintain audit logs for all critical operations
  - Annual third-party audit required for certification

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- **Edition:** 2022
- **Current as of:** January 2026
- **Canonical Source:** [ISO 27001](https://www.iso.org/standard/27001)
- **Project Obligations:**
  - Establish ISMS (Information Security Management System)
  - Risk assessment and treatment
  - Access control policies
  - Cryptographic controls

### GDPR
- **Version:** Regulation (EU) 2016/679
- **Current as of:** January 2026
- **Canonical Source:** [EUR-Lex GDPR](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj)
- **Project Obligations:**
  - Lawful basis for processing (consent or legitimate interest)
  - Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
  - Data minimization and purpose limitation
  - 72-hour breach notification
  - Data Protection Impact Assessment for high-risk processing

### CCPA/CPRA
- **Version:** California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by CPRA)
- **Current as of:** January 2026
- **Canonical Source:** [CA Attorney General CCPA](https://oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa)
- **Project Obligations:**
  - Right to know, delete, and opt-out
  - Privacy policy disclosures
  - Service provider contractual requirements

### HIPAA (Conditional)
- **Version:** Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (1996, HITECH Act updates)
- **Current as of:** January 2026
- **Canonical Source:** [HHS HIPAA](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/index.html)
- **Project Obligations:** (Only if PHI is processed)
  - Business Associate Agreement with customers
  - Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
  - Minimum necessary standard
  - Breach notification within 60 days
